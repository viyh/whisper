• Whisper

1. Type/paste your secret text or select a file to upload.
2. Copy the autogenerated password or type your own.
3. Choose an expiration period.
4. Click Create Secret. A secret link will be generated.
5. Send the link and password (separately) to anyone that needs the secret.
6. The secret can be retrieved by clicking the link and entering the password.
